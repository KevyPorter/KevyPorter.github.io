Features

Find out the many interesting features of this mod here!

NOTE:

Ever HUDs position and colors can be customized with the key press of 'Right-Shift'

These features can be also seen in-game by using the /pphelp command.

/CheckUpdates

This feature shows you if Pixel+ has any updates avaliable!

/booster queue

This revamped /booster queue allows you to /tip players by clicking on them.

/names (player)

View a certain players' certain name or names in-game. (Uses Mojang API)

/ppafk (message)

An auto message sender to a player who tries to message you when you are afk.

/ppstats (player)

View a player's stats in-game without having to go on the website. (Uses Hypixel's Public API)

/togglechat (main, party, guild, pms, shouts)

Toggles the specific chat on or off.

Armor Damage Reduction

Tells you how much your armor, armor enchants and potion effects will reduce the amount of damage dealt to you. (Displayed in the Info Hud)

Shift and Right-Click

Suggests adding the person you are looking at as a friend or sending them a message

/goal (game) (type) (amount)

Set a goal in any gamemode (only blitz at the moment) and set a personal goal for yourself. For e.x Kills.

Info Hud

This hud shows: FPS, Time, Ping to the Server, and Armor Reduction.

Armor Hud

Shows the current armor you are wearing and the weapon you are holding in a customizable hud.

Effect Hud

Shows active potion effects, the amplifier and how long the potion lasts in a customizable hud.

Friends: Quick friend request

Shift Right-CLick a player to prompt adding them as a friend or message them.

Friends: Quick remove or message

In your /f list, quickly message someone or remove them from your friends list with the click of a button!

Report: Easy report

On /report (name) (reason) a link will show in your chat that copies a report to your clipboard. Go to the forums and easily past it into a report and show the staff!
